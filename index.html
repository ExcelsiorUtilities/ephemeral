Ephemeral

Lightweight, offline, agile task management made simple.

Why Ephemeral?

Ephemeral helps you manage your sprints efficiently without unnecessary clutter. Track time, stay offline, and generate meaningful reports — all without login friction.

Features

  • Stopwatches for each task
  • Offline operation — no internet required
  • Lightweight and fast
  • No login required
  • Sprint / Agile based workflow
  • Generates detailed reports
  • Sprint statistics at a glance
  • Shows estimated vs actual task times
  • Option to make tasks recurrent for each sprint
  • Option to discontinue tasks to reduce stopwatch clutter
