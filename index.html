Ephemeral

Simple time tracking utility for Agile environments.

Why Ephemeral?

Ephemeral helps you manage your sprints efficiently without unnecessary clutter. Track time, stay offline, and generate meaningful reports — all with a few clicks.

  • Are you confident with your timesheet estimates after a day of constant interruptions?
  • Are you comfortable remembering yesterday's issues during standup?
  • Do you know how many hours your story points are?
  • Are you happy with your team giving you the side-eye for oversizing your stories at planning?

    • No?

    Ephemeral is a solution!

  • Ephemeral is free/donationware
  • Quick setup — up and running in moments
  • Lightweight and fast
  • Offline - no account creation or login

    • No more timesheet guesswork!

    • Ultra precise task time measurement
    • Stopwatch for each task
    • Forget to switch stopwatches? Easily move time from one stopwatch to another!
    • Task times are consolidated by common charge numbers for easy timesheet entry
    • Timesheet rounding to 1/10 or 1/4 hour
    • Automatic stopwatch cleanup for completed tasks
    • Workday completion notification

    Refine your planning process and be prepared for retrospectives!

    • Discover which tasks are over- and under-estimated.
    • Easily see workday history for any sprint.
    • Generate sprint report at the click of a button.
